About

I chose to be a designer because I love connecting ideas, solving problems, and communicating messages. There’s almost nothing more satisfying than creating a great piece of work that has a purpose behind it. Design-wise I am fascinated with geometry, negative space, pattern, and colour. I prefer work that is clean, functional and based on strong concepts. However, I am always learning and believe it’s important to grow and try new things. I am a well-rounded designer with talents in illustrating, branding, and typography. I like to put my best effort into everything I do and work hard to meet goals.
